The Importance of Networking

October 2024 Career Development

What is Networking? Why it is so important to get involved in the creative industries!

In simple terms, networking means interacting with others to exchange information that will be helpful for your career. This typically means talking to others about your work and swapping contact details to stay in touch for job opportunities or ways to collaborate.

Whether you're just starting out, or fresh out of Uni, networking can significantly aid your first or next steps within a career. Networking is crucial in the creative industry, therefore, it is important that you learn how to connect with potential employers and other developers both online and offline.
